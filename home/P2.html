Drawing Performance

Drawing Performance


Spring 2017

This project aims to cerate connections between two section cuts taken from two motion drawings. The purpose of the connections is to represent the motion of the two tools. Some words for the motions of the tools are: grab, deform, transfer, rotate, lift, etc. There are three major parts of the sculpture: the base, solid slices, and grids going through slices and the base as a supporting system. Part of the base is elevated, inplying a transition from the ground. The slices starts from the ground and gradually transfer to the top of the base, implying the transition force applied to the base. The length of the grids are also manipulated to show some curves.
